WhiteCross Prison Regulations

Welcome to WhiteCross

As a member of WhiteCross prison, you are expected to follow all rules to maintain order and fairness. Breaking rules may lead to punishments or removal from the game.

Prison Rules

  • 1. Respect staff: Always follow directions from guards and staff members.
  • 2. No cheating: Exploits, hacks, or unfair advantage is strictly prohibited.
  • 3. No fighting: PvP is not allowed inside the prison unless part of an official event.
  • 4. No griefing: Do not block, trap, or harm other players unnecessarily.
  • 5. Follow roleplay rules: Stay in character and respect the prison setting.
  • 6. No spam or advertising: Keep chat clean and relevant to the game.
  • 7. Report issues properly: Use the official channels to report bugs or rule violations.

Consequences

Breaking the rules can lead to:

  • Warnings
  • Temporary jail or kick from the game
  • Permanent ban in extreme cases
